about

OHAMS INTERNATIONAL SERVICES LIMITED is a leading provider of international services, procurement solutions, and oil and gas expertise. With a strong global presence and a proven track record, we offer a comprehensive range of services to support our clients' operations and facilitate their success in complex and competitive industries.

International Services

Our international services division specializes in assisting businesses in expanding their operations and reaching new markets around the world. We provide comprehensive market research, strategic planning, and logistical support to help our clients navigate the challenges of global expansion. From market entry strategies to establishing distribution networks, we offer tailored solutions to drive international growth and maximize opportunities.

Procurement Solutions

Our procurement division is dedicated to streamlining supply chain processes and optimizing procurement strategies for our clients. Leveraging our extensive network of trusted suppliers and industry partnerships, we ensure efficient sourcing, competitive pricing, and reliable delivery of goods and services. We specialize in procurement across various sectors, including manufacturing, construction, energy, and technology, delivering cost-effective solutions that meet quality standards and project timelines.

Oil and Gas Expertise

With deep-rooted expertise in the oil and gas industry, we provide comprehensive services to support exploration, production, and infrastructure projects. Our team of industry professionals offers project management, engineering, procurement, and construction management (EPCM) services, ensuring the successful execution of oil and gas projects from inception to completion. We are committed to adhering to the highest safety standards and environmental practices while delivering innovative and sustainable solutions to our clients.

Partnerships and Collaborations

We believe in the power of partnerships and collaborations to drive success. We have established strong relationships with industry-leading organizations, suppliers, and service providers, enabling us to deliver comprehensive and integrated solutions to our clients. By working closely with our partners, we leverage collective expertise and resources to achieve exceptional outcomes and exceed client expectations.

Corporate Social Responsibility

As a responsible corporate citizen, we are committed to making a positive impact in communities where we operate. We actively engage in social initiatives, environmental stewardship, and community development programs. Our goal is to contribute to sustainable growth, empower local communities, and promote social well-being through our operations and partnerships.
